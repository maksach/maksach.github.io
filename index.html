About Me

I'm a data and ML engineer with years of experience building data pipelines, ETL systems, and big data applications at scale. A strong DevOps foundation keeps everything I build reliable and production-ready, and pursuing my Master's in Computer Science part-time has let me go deep into AI and ML — a space I've become genuinely obsessed with.

If there's a way to automate something, I've probably already tried it. From work pipelines to travel planning to personal organization, I love using technology to cut the friction out of everyday life.
